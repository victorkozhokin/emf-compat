# EMF Compat: Better Combat

![Tridnet Attack!](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/Smytm33T/images/a8c92fd948fda83e1d1a57666baaf538901bea74.gif)

A small client-side mod that makes **[Better Combat](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/better-combat-by-daedelus)** attack animations work correctly with **[Entity Model Features](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/entity-model-features)** player models.

Tested with **[Fresh Animations: Player Extension](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/texture-packs/fa-player-extension)** but it should work with any player animation resource pack.

Looks very good with [Visceral Combat](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/visceral-combat)

## Features

- Compatible with **[Fresh Animations: Player Extension](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/texture-packs/fa-player-extension)**.
- Your weapon swings and charge poses look right instead of being overwritten by idle resource-pack animations.
- Body and legs keep their EMF animations during attacks; only arms switch to Better Combat.
- Held items align with your swing in first person, so weapons don't float next to your arm.
- Smooth hand-off back to EMF at the end of an attack, no single-frame snap.
- Works with one-handed, two-handed and mace attacks.
